    • About

    Sireum Logika is both a highly automated and interactive verification framework for Slang that strongly emphasizes scalability and usability, offering incremental and (massively) parallel verification integrated in an industrial-grade IDE – Sireum IVE. Logika can be used: (a) without up-front specification costs to (interprocedurally) verify Slang programs up to some user-configurable bounds, (b) with behavioral contracts to compositionally verify Slang programs for further scalability, or (c) in a hybrid interprocedural/compositional verification mode.
